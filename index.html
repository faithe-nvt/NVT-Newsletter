📧 Email Sending — EmailJS (free, 200/month)

Setup steps:

  1. Go to emailjs.com and sign up free
  2. Go to Email Services → Add Service → choose Gmail → connect your Gmail account → copy the Service ID
  3. Go to Email Templates → Create Template → set the body to just: {{{html_content}}} and subject to {{subject}} → Save → copy the Template ID
  4. Go to Account → copy your Public Key

Emails send from whichever Gmail account you connected in EmailJS.
